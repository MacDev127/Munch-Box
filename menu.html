Legenderry Burger

$5 - $20
The world-famous Legenderry Burger is as flexible as it is desirable. The Legenderry is actually a beefburger (the beef is there’s no ham) and consists of a freshly-ground, 100% Irish beef patty, cooked medium in a normal soft bun with a slight glazing on top. Then layered with iceberg lettuce, fresh tomato, onions, pickles and house dressing. Then mustard, mayo or ketchup to taste

Chicken Chop Suey

Chicken Chop Suey (which means ‘assorted pieces’) is a main containing…well, assorted pieces of chicken (or beef, prawns, pork and vegetables). All stir-fried rapidly with eggs, bean sprouts, cabbage, celery and bound in thick tasty sauce – then delivered chop-chop, by us. Singapore Fried Noodles will have your taste buds singing thanks to vermicelli noodles seasoned with curry powder, veggies, scrambled eggs and a mix of chicken, beef, char siu pork or prawns.

Margherita

$5 - $20
Every Italian city has created a pizza to be proud of. Pizza Margherita is the famed Neapolitan icon made from San Marzano tomatoes (red), buffalo mozzarella (white) and fresh basil (green) to echo an edible Italian Flag. The quintessential Sicilian Pizza is the beloved Capricciosa, aka the Grandma Pizza as it’s one of the oldest styles,originally thick-crusted with tomato sauce, mozzarella, ‘shrooms, anchovies, onions, ham, artichokes and olives.

Tacos

$5 - $20
Tacos have come a long ways since their Aztec days. Many American chefs have put their own spin on this Mexican dish and made it their own. You might see duck confit tacos with a cherry reduction sauce as a menu item at fancy restaurants. Have you tasted Korean BBQ tacos from a food truck? What about a chocolate taco shell with ice cream filling for dessert?4 Whether it is authentic Mexican tacos al pastor or tofu-stuffed tacos, they are all worth trying and all worth exploring. They will soon have you saying… ¡Que rico!
