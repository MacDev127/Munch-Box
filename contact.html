Customer Service Enquiries

We're here to help! The fastest way to get an answer is to use the app or website. Simply log in to your account, select a recent order and then use Order help to report your query. Or you can mail us at support@munchbox.co.uk. Please note we don't accept orders over the phone - so if you'd like to place an order, please place it online.
